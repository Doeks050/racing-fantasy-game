# Race Simulation

Race results are calculated from:

- Driver Stats
- Car Base Stats
- Installed Parts Stats
- Team Stats
- Circuit Stats
- Sector 1 Stats
- Sector 2 Stats
- Sector 3 Stats
- Race Events

## Basic Formula
Total Race Score =
Sector 1 Score
+ Sector 2 Score
+ Sector 3 Score
+ Pit Strategy Score
+ Reliability Score
- Mistake Penalty
- Tyre Degradation Penalty

## Sector Based Racing
Each circuit has 3 sectors. Every sector has its own stat weights.

This means the best setup changes per race weekend.
